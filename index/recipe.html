Blondies

Ingredients:

  1. 1 1/2 sticks of melted, cooled, un-salted butter
  2. 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  3. 1/2 tablespoons of kosher salt
  4. 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  5. 1 1/2 cups of packed dark brown sugar
  6. 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  7. 2 eggs lightly beaten
  8. Flakey sea salt
  9. 1 cup + 2 tablespoons of bittersweet chocolate chips
  10. 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts(optional)
  11. 9x13 inch pan

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Butter the 9x13in pan, line pan with parchement paper, then butter parchment.
  3. Whisk the flour, baking powder, and salt together in a medium bowl.
  4. In another bowl, whisk the melted butter and brown sugar together until combined.
  5. Add the vanilla and eggs till combined.
  6. Gentle mix flour mixture into wet ingredients, careful, do not overmix.
  7. Mix in 1 cup of chocolate chips and walnuts(optional).
  8. Pour batter into 9x13in pan and scatter remaining chocolate ontop of settled batter.
  9. Bake until top is golden brown, approximately 20-25 minutes.
  10. Let cool before removing from pan.
  11. Batch should yeild 16 blondies.
